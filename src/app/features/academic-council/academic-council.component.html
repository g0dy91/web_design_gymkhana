Academic
Council






The Academic Affairs Council (AAC) promotes and addresses Academic related issues. The Academic Affairs Secretary is the Chairman of this Council and leads, organizes and coordinates all its activities.
      The members of the Council are the Class representatives of all the Departments. A team of 5 students is also formed to be part of SUGC, to contribute ideas and assist the student senate members in matters and discussion related to SUGC. The Council assists the Academic Affairs Secretary in the preparation of the agenda related to Academics, have regular meetings, problems/concerns related to Academics, and have regular meetings with the Faculty, Dean and Director regarding the same.

Student Gymkhana IITH is the student body of IIT Hyderabad which aims to bring the students together for their mutual all-round development. All the members of the Student Gymkhana are bounded by the Student Gymkhana Constitution and functions through its Executive Council, with the guidance of faculty incharges.
