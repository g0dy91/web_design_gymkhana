Media
Council







The media council of IITH was formed in May 2014 and is a student council that helps in taking IITH to every individual in and outside IITH. The council is involved in publicizing the institute through social media, social events, etc. The Media Council is currently involved in various newsletters of IITH like the academic newsletter, placement newsletter and works under the guidance of various faculties of IITH.
      The council is also involved in managing all the social contacts of IITH. Apart from this, a Student Activity Page (SAP) that updates everyone of IITH’s daily students’ activities is also maintained by the council. The Media Council is also responsible for updating the Student Gymkhana Website with all the important documents and latest information.
